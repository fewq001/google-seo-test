History of Classic Cars

Early Years (1880s-1920s)

Karl Benz’s 1885 car began the auto era; Ford’s Model T (1908) made cars affordable for the masses.

Golden Age (1930s-1950s)

The 1950s brought chrome-heavy designs like the Cadillac Eldorado, reflecting post-war prosperity.

Muscle Cars (1960s-1970s)

The Ford Mustang defined the powerful 1960s muscle car era, blending style and performance.
